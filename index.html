Trimstone Manor

A beautiful, historic, North Devon manor house getaway, comprising of self catering holiday cottages,

comfortable hotel rooms, swimming pool, games room and beautiful gardens.

We are centrally located for easy access to award winning local beaches such as Woolacombe and Saunton Sands.

As well as quaint coastal towns and villages such as Ilfracombe, Lee, Mortehoe, Lynton and Lynmouth just minutes away.

We are situated within an area of outstanding natural beauty benefiting from stunning clifftop walks, nature reserves and Exmoor national park.
