Trimstone Manor

Guest House and Holiday Cottages

Step back in time at our beautiful, historic North Devon manor house, offering a quintessential British holiday experience.

Whether you prefer the indulgence of our comfortable rooms or the freedom of a charming self-catering cottage, every stay includes access to superb amenities like our indoor swimming pool, dedicated games room, and peaceful, sweeping gardens with the addition of our Emus, peacocks, ducks and poultry.

Our central location is unbeatable. We are the perfect base for exploring the region's best, surf the award-winning waves at Woolacombe and Saunton Sands, wander the quaint streets of Ilfracombe and Mortehoe, or explore the dramatic scenery of Exmoor National Park. We are situated within an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty and surrounded by stunning coastal trails.

To guarantee the best price and avoid all third-party commission—always book your stay directly with us.
